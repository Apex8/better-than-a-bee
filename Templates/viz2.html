Honey Production and Pesticide Use

One team goal was to attempt to find factors that influenced a decrease in honey bees, bee colonies, or honey production. When analyzing the Honeybees and Neonic Pesticides data, I noticed that there was a slight downward trend in honey production per colony, as well as total production. At the same time, there was an increase in most of the types of pesticides recorded in this data source. It seems that there may be a relation to pesticide use and bee colony health and honey production.
